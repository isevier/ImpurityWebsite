Donate to Impurity

Donating to the server helps keep the server running. All donations are highly appreciated and go towards the monthly server bill of $30 USD. By donating, you can recieve an in-game perk to change the color of your name, and you will also recieve, by choice, a role as a donator in the official Impurity discord.
